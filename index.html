Ronda, North Carolina, USA

Location

Ronda is located in Wilkes County, North Carolina. The town is roughly 60 miles west of Appalachian State University.

About

Weather

Humidity is typically high with mild winters and extreme summers.

Population

The population as of 2000 was ~560 residents.

Education

There is one high school (East Wilkes High) and one elementary school (Ronda Clingman Elementary).

Things To Do

  • Visit the farmers market
  • Exercise at the local park
  • Fuel up at the only gas station in town
